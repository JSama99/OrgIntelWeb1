Visual direction:

Create a polished, stylized Afro-futurist Black male OrgIntel Architect. He represents the visitor operating inside the Intelligence Headquarters—not a specific founder likeness.

The design must include:

- Believable adult humanoid proportions
- Clearly readable head, neck, torso, shoulders, arms, hands, legs, and boots
- Warm dark-brown skin tone
- Short, shaped black hair
- Visible eyes, brows, nose, mouth, ears, and jawline
- Calm, intelligent expression
- Black architectural jacket or short coat
- Dark fitted trousers
- Cyan inner panel and controlled cyan piping
- Restrained gold collar, wrist, and jacket accents
- Structured shoulders and layered clothing
- Gloves or clearly modeled hands
- Futuristic black boots with subtle cyan details
- A recognizable silhouette at normal gameplay distance
- No spherical placeholder anatomy
- No oversized emissive aura
- No weapons
- No logos requiring text textures

Style:

- Premium stylized realism
- Smooth, intentional forms
- Slightly heroic proportions without becoming cartoonishly muscular
- Consistent with the cyan, gold, black, and deep-navy OrgIntel environment
- PBR materials with restrained roughness, metalness, and emissive values
- Emissive accents must remain subtle enough to avoid bloom clipping

Geometry and runtime requirements:

- Maximum 12,000 rendered triangles
- Prefer 6,000–10,000 triangles
- Minimize mesh and material count

- Character height should be approximately 1.8 scene units before runtime scaling
- Face the direction compatible with the existing runtime rotationY Math.PI

- Enable sensible castShadow and receiveShadow behavior through the existing loader
- Avoid transparent materials unless necessary for the eyes
- Avoid external texture dependencies for this first review; use embedded PBR material values
