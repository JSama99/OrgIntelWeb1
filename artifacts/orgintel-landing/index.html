What OrgIntel does

Three things every business needs. Most have none.

A management layer that sits above your agents and tools — remembering, reasoning, and proving on your behalf.

Every artifact your agents and tools produce — captured automatically and connected to the work it came from. Ask what happened; get the answer with receipts. No filing. No tagging. No forgetting.
